Testing Machines

X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) Test Machines

Is a non-destructive analytical technique used to determine the elemental composition of materials. XRF analyzers determine the chemistry of a sample by measuring the fluorescent (or secondary) X-ray emitted from a sample when it is excited by a primary X-ray source.

Box Compression Test (BCT) Machine

Use to test the maximum force that shows the ability of box to withstand the compression.

Use in determining the vertical strength and stacking level.

Chamber Test Machine

Used in reconditioning the corr. Boxes into specific degree centigrade & relative humidity for any required time.

Bursting Test Machine

Use to test the ability of combined board to withstand a constant pressure before breaking.

Drying Oven/Weighing Scale

Use for checking paper combination.

Cobb

Determines the amount of water absorbed into the surface by a sized (non-bibulous) paper, paperboard, and corrugated fibreboard paper or paperboard sample in a set period of time.

EDGE CRUSH TESTER

The Edge Crush test is a laboratory test method that is used to measure the cross-direction crushing of a sample of corrugated board. It gives information on the ability of a particular board construction to resist crushing.
