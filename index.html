    Our Innovation

    Hiple Ace

    As a high performance packing material, HIPLE-ACE offers exceptional performance in a diverse range of applications for transportof machinery, automotive parts, computer components and industrial and consumer electronics.

    Hiple Ace

    Is a light and strong High Performance Double-Wall & Triple-Wall Corrugated Fiberboard.

    • Delivers maximum strength and an alternative packaging material to wood or steel
    • Reduce weight (to reduce freight cost)
    • Design flexibility
    • Lightweight and easy to assemble
    • 100% recyclable, environmental friendly packaging material
